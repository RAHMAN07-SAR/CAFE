ABOUT US

Our Story — Craft Coffee & Community

We have been serving small-batch roasted coffee and handmade pastries with love in Bengaluru. From the bean to the cup, we focus on quality, sustainability, and a warm experience for every guest.

Our Mission Meet the team

From single-origin espresso to seasonal pastries — crafted daily by our team.

OUR MISSION

Quality Coffee, Ethical Sourcing, Local Community

Founded in 2016, Cafe Bengaluru began as a small neighbourhood shop with the goal of bringing transparent sourcing and quality roasting to local coffee lovers. We build relationships with farmers, roast in small batches, and train our baristas to extract the best flavours from each roast.

Sustainability

We partner with smallholder farms and invest in ethical trade practices.

Community

Events, workshops and local collaborations to strengthen neighbourhood ties.

Craftsmanship

Small-batch roasting and precise brewing for consistent quality.

Transparency

Clear traceability of beans and honest menus.
